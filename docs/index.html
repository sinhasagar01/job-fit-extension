How it works

Five steps. A few seconds.

No copy-pasting, no dashboards, no sign-up. Open a job, ask one question, get a real answer.

1

Upload your résumé

Add your PDF once. It's parsed locally and reused for every job you check — nothing uploaded.

2

Open any job listing

Navigate to a posting on LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, or any job page.

3

Click "Analyze fit"

One tap in the toolbar popup. JobFit reads the job description straight from the page.

4

See your score

A color-coded 1–10 score with a radar chart breaking down fit across five dimensions.

5

Act on the gaps

Concrete, prioritized steps grounded in your actual resume and the real job description.

What you get

An honest read on every role.

Most tools give you a number. JobFit shows you the reasoning — and what to do about it.

Explainable scoring

A fit score from 1 to 10 across five weighted dimensions — skills, experience, domain, keywords, and education — shown as a clear radar breakdown.

A real action plan

Not vague advice. Specific, prioritized steps grounded in the actual job description and your actual resume — what to learn, build, or add.

Private by design

Your resume is parsed on your device and never touches our servers — because we don't have any. Nothing stored, nothing tracked, nothing sold.

The difference

The one job tool that can't leak your resume.

Most resume tools upload your career history to a server you'll never see. JobFit takes a different path: everything happens inside your browser.

  • Your resume PDF is read and parsed locally — it never gets uploaded to us.
  • Scoring goes straight from your browser to the AI provider you choose, with your own key.
  • We run no servers, keep no accounts, and collect zero analytics. There's nothing to breach.
